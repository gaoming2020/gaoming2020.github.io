The core of this strategy lies in identifying specific market imbalances, which are known as Fair Value Gaps (FVGs). These are essentially areas on a price chart where price action seems to have moved very quickly, creating a ‘gap’ where orders weren’t fully filled. The real magic happens when you start looking at this in combination with other patterns. Here’s the lowdown:

  • Spotting the Setup: The first thing you need to do is look at the one-hour timeframe for an existing high timeframe Fair Value Gap. A clear gap in price action on the chart is the first sign. The video highlights when price has come down and is showing signs of respecting the FVG. This is our foundation and a bullish signal.

  • The Liquidity Grab and FVG Combo: After identifying the higher timeframe gap, drop down to a lower timeframe, the video recommends the five-minute chart, for your entry. We are now looking for very specific signs. First is a grab of liquidity to the downside. Next, we look at the the price action that has occurred around our liquidity grab. Look for a bearish fair value gap, then observe if a bullish fair value gap was created in this area. These are all bullish signs. This formation is key, and its reliability makes it a valuable strategy for traders. You wait for the price to come down and enter this fair value gap.

  • Profit and Risk Management: Now, where should you take profit? Using your Fibonacci retracement tool you should mark from the liquidity grab to the recent high. Your take profit zone should be at the standard deviation of 2. And your stop loss should be placed just below the recent low.

Trading can be a rollercoaster, but having a strategy like this can give you a clear edge. It’s not about blindly following a pattern; it’s about understanding the underlying mechanics of the market. Say Goodbye to Back Pain: The One Exercise You Need

Posted on 2024-12-19

Tired of nagging back pain? You’re not alone. Millions suffer from it daily, often turning to temporary fixes. But what if I told you there’s a simple yet incredibly effective exercise that can provide long-lasting relief? Forget complicated routines and expensive equipment; this one move targets the core and back muscles for a powerful, pain-busting effect.

Let’s dive in. Start on your hands and knees, making sure your back is straight. Now, here’s the key: gently pull your belly button towards your spine, engaging your core muscles. This subtle move is crucial for stability. With your core engaged, slowly lift your knees just an inch or two off the floor, creating a hover. Feel those muscles start to fire up? That’s your core and back working together. Now, for the real game-changer. Keep that hover and alternate gently tapping one knee down towards the floor and then raising it again. Repeat on the other side. You will immediately feel the intensity increase, engaging even more of those lower back and hip muscles.

The beauty of this exercise lies in its simplicity. It doesn’t require any special equipment, and you can do it in the comfort of your own home. The slow, controlled movements are key. Don’t rush it! Aim for 10-15 repetitions on each side, focusing on maintaining a stable core and controlled movements. The best part? You’re not just alleviating pain; you’re building a stronger, more resilient back that’s less prone to injury. If you feel good after the first set, go for another round. It’s all about listening to your body and progressively challenging yourself.

Unlock Your Pull-Up Potential: A 10-Level Guide from Beginner to Elite

Posted on 2024-12-19

Think you’ve mastered the pull-up? Think again! Most of us stop at just a basic pull-up, but what if I told you there’s a whole ladder of progression leading to some seriously impressive feats of strength? We’re not just talking about adding a few extra reps. We’re talking about a journey from your very first pull-up to full-blown front lever mastery.

Let’s dive into a 10-level guide to transform your pull-up game, inspired by a killer workout video. Whether you’re struggling to get your chin over the bar or you’re a seasoned athlete, there’s something here for you.

Level 1: The Foundation - Strict Pull-Ups
This is where it all starts. We’re talking proper form: chin over the bar, full range of motion from a dead hang. If you’re not quite there yet, focus on exercises like hanging from the bar, scapular shrugs, and body rows. Think of this as building a solid foundation. Aim for seven perfect reps to advance. And yes, going higher than chin-over-bar is a bonus!

Level 2: Adding Resistance - Weighted Pull-Ups
Ready to take it up a notch? Adding weight is a game-changer. Start with a moderate weight and be patient. It’ll feel tough initially, but over time, you’ll adapt. This is how you make standard pull-ups feel effortless. Aim for six weighted pull-ups with solid form before moving on.

Level 3: Explosive Power - Clapping Pull-Ups
Now we’re getting into the fun stuff! After training with weights, you’ll feel so light you can add a clap between reps. This is all about explosive power and control. Even if it feels a little scary letting go of the bar, start with a ground tap test. Nail 5-6 reps, and you’re ready for level four.

Level 4-10: The Journey to Elite - Pullovers, High Pull-Ups, Tuck Front Lever, One Arm Negatives, One Arm Pull-Ups, and Front Lever Pull-Ups
These levels take you from pullovers and high pull ups to the most challenging moves like one-arm pull ups and the full front lever pull ups. Each step builds on the last, demanding increased strength, body control, and technique. For example, the pullover is a gateway to bar freestyling and the negative one-arm pull-up builds strength for the full move. If you get stuck, don’t be afraid to regress to earlier levels and use drop sets (going from a harder level to an easier one) to break through plateaus. The key to progressing is to stay consistent, and listen to your body.

The Takeaway:
The pull-up isn’t just one exercise, it’s a pathway. By understanding and working through each level, you’ll not only build strength, but also gain impressive control and a better understanding of your own body. Ready to start?
