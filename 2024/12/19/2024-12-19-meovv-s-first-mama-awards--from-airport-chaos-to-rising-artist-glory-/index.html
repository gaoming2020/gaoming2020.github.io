MEOVV's First MAMA Awards: From Airport Chaos to Rising Artist Glory!

Posted on 2024-12-19

Ever wondered what it’s like for a rising K-pop group to attend a major awards show like MAMA? Well, MEOVV just took us behind the scenes, and let me tell you, it’s a wild ride! From sleepy airplane arrivals to receiving their first award, their journey was filled with excitement, a touch of chaos, and a whole lot of love for their fans.

Pre-Show Hype & Hotel Shenanigans

The video starts with the girls on their way to Osaka, buzzing with anticipation. It wasn’t all glamorous, though. There was the struggle of jet lag, hurried practice sessions with Japanese dancers, and the hunt for that perfect cold room to sleep in. But even in the chaos, their personalities shone through. One member worried her love for hot baths would make the others catch a cold, showing just how considerate they are of each other. This genuine care for each other makes them so likable.

Convenience Store Chaos & Fan Love

Beyond the hotel walls, they hit up a convenience store, something that was both normal and exciting for them at the same time. The girls were so thrilled, it was as if they found hidden treasure, and it was hilarious! Their genuine excitement is incredibly contagious! The airport was another highlight, despite their shyness. While they couldn’t greet all fans, their heartfelt message through the camera showed how much they appreciate the love and support.

MAMA Magic & Award Glory

The energy really ramped up when they arrived at the stadium. Nerves were high, but so was excitement. Their hard work paid off when they received the “Favorite Rising Artist” award! The girls were completely shocked, and the genuine excitement, mixed with a dose of disbelief, made it even more special. It is incredibly heart-warming to see their dreams being fulfilled right in front of our eyes! They were determined to “break sumn” on stage the following day. The video shows how much hard work goes behind making these performances happen.

A New Member & A Night to Remember

Adding to the fun, they introduced their “6th member” (a stuffed animal!), showering it with love and care, planning everything from its skincare routine to its walking schedule. The backstage also included silly moments with a managers gift and lots of chocolate! Overall, their journey was filled with laughter, teamwork, and shared joy, which was then amplified during their performances! From eye contact with the fans to the overall energy, it’s clear that MEOVV is a group to watch. MEOVV’s MAMA experience is a reminder that with hard work, dedication, and a little bit of chaos, amazing things can happen. If you weren’t a fan before, their genuine joy and talent will absolutely win you over.

So, what are you waiting for? Check out MEOVV’s stage at MAMA if you haven’t already and join their growing fanbase! Share your favorite moment of their video in the comments below, and let’s show MEOVV some love!
