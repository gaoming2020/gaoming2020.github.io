Why Tech CEOs are Suddenly Cozying Up to Trump: It's Not What You Think

Posted on 2024-12-19

Remember when tech CEOs seemed to be on a different planet than Donald Trump? Well, buckle up, because the landscape has dramatically shifted. It’s not about love, it’s about strategy, pure and simple. The wave of tech titans, from Mark Zuckerberg to Tim Cook, flocking to meet with Trump is making headlines, and it begs the question: What’s really going on?

The answer, according to Kevin O’Leary, is surprisingly straightforward. It boils down to two key factors: power and profits. First, these CEOs recognize Trump’s mandate. He didn’t just win the Electoral College; he also secured the popular vote. They understand that he’s a force to be reckoned with, at least for the next two years. Ignoring that reality would be a foolish move.

Second, and perhaps more importantly, it’s about money. Trump’s administration essentially guarantees a 21% corporate tax rate, and for these tech giants, that’s a sweet deal. Add to that the prospect of federal deregulation and smoother merger and acquisition processes, and suddenly a meeting with Trump starts to seem less like a political statement and more like smart business. They’re playing the game to ensure that their bottom lines are well protected, and who can blame them?

But what about the concerns around things like TikTok? O’Leary points out that Trump’s recent comments about the app were far from an endorsement. He is likely not going to ignore the bipartisan concerns of Chinese spyware. There is a very real prospect of a forced sale to American investors. So, while the tech world might be making a show of warming up to Trump, it’s not necessarily a shift in ideological views but a calculated move to safeguard their interests. They are, as O’Leary puts it, “sucking up” because he’s the man in power, at least for now.

This sudden embrace of Trump by tech leaders reveals a lot about the dynamics between business and politics. It’s a reminder that when it comes down to it, the business world will navigate the political landscape to optimize their growth, regardless of previous affiliations.
