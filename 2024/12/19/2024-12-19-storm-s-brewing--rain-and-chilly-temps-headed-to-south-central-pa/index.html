Storm's Brewing: Rain and Chilly Temps Headed to South-Central PA

Posted on 2024-12-19

Ever feel like the weather’s a rollercoaster? One minute you’re enjoying a mild day, and the next you’re bundling up for a cold snap. Well, folks in south-central Pennsylvania are in for just that! Following a period of clear skies, prepare for increasing cloud cover, a shift from the balmy temperatures, and a chance of rain.

Here’s what you need to know:

  • Cloudy Skies Ahead: Don’t get too comfortable with that sunshine. We’re seeing increasing cloud cover this morning, and it’s all thanks to a low-pressure system making its way toward the Susquehanna Valley. This means a transition from a clear, calm morning to a potentially soggy afternoon.

  • Rain is Coming: Get those umbrellas ready! The storm system is expected to bring rain, starting in the afternoon and potentially stretching into the evening hours. The rain will move west to east, so residents in eastern counties might not see it until later. While the rainfall isn’t expected to be torrential – only a tenth or two of an inch is predicted, mostly in southwestern PA - it’s enough to warrant caution. Prepare for potentially slick roads during your commute.

  • Cold Snap on the Way: This system isn’t just bringing rain; it’s ushering in a wave of colder air. Temperatures will drop significantly as we head toward the weekend. Highs will fall from the upper 40s today to the low 40s tomorrow. By the weekend, we’ll be seeing highs in the 20s! Factor in a blustery breeze, and it will feel like single digits - it’s time to dig out those winter coats and gloves!

This weather shift is a reminder of how quickly things can change. Keep an eye on your local forecasts, check your credit card statements for any signs of fraud this holiday season, and prepare for some cold weather on the way. Stay safe and stay dry – and share this post with your friends to keep them in the loop too!
