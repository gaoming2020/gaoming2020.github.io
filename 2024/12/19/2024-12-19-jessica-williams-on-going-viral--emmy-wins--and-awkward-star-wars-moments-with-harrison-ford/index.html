Jessica Williams on Going Viral, Emmy Wins, and Awkward Star Wars Moments with Harrison Ford

Posted on 2024-12-19

Ever had one of those moments where you’re just living for someone else’s success, and it accidentally turns into an internet sensation? Jessica Williams knows the feeling! In her recent appearance on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon, she hilariously recounted her reaction to Ayo Edebiri’s Emmy win – a moment that went viral, thanks to her unbridled joy. But that’s not all; she also shared some behind-the-scenes stories from her time on the show ‘Shrinking,’ including an adorably awkward encounter with the legendary Harrison Ford.

Let’s dive in! First up: the Emmy win. Jessica’s reaction was the kind of pure, unfiltered enthusiasm we all wish we had on camera. Forget polite applause, she was the human embodiment of a celebration emoji. It’s that kind of genuine emotion that connects with viewers – and boy, did it connect! It’s a great reminder that sometimes, the best moments are the ones where you let your happiness shine through, even if it ends up on the internet for all to see.

Then, things got hilariously awkward (in the best way possible). Apparently, while filming ‘Shrinking,’ Jessica found herself talking to Harrison Ford… and accidentally brought up Star Wars. Imagine being in the presence of Han Solo himself, only to nervously blurt out something about the galaxy far, far away. We’ve all been there, right? It’s a reminder that even our heroes are just people, and sometimes the most relatable thing they can do is be a little bit dorky. Jessica’s description of the encounter is a perfect blend of relatable awkwardness and starstruck admiration.

Finally, Jessica touched on her new hobby: the guitar! She mentions ‘soft quitting’ which many people can relate to in their journeys. All in all it sounds like Jessica is living a life of new experiences and sharing them with the world. So, if you’re looking for a little laugh and some relatable celebrity stories, Jessica Williams has you covered! And hey, maybe it’s time to pick up that long-forgotten hobby, even if you soft-quit after a couple of days.
