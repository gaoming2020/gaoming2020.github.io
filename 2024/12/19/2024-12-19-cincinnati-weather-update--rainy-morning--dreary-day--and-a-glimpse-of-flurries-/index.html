Cincinnati Weather Update: Rainy Morning, Dreary Day, and a Glimpse of Flurries!

Posted on 2024-12-19

Ever feel like the weather is playing a cruel joke? Well, Cincinnati, it seems like we’re all in on it together! We’re trading in the sunshine for a good dose of rain this morning, and trust me, it’s not exactly a tropical downpour. Think more along the lines of a persistent, ‘I’m not going anywhere’ kind of drizzle.

Morning Commute Blues

This morning’s rain isn’t just a sprinkle; it’s the kind that makes you question your life choices while stuck in traffic. The radar shows rain showers moving throughout Greater Cincinnati, with some of the heavier downpours hitting southeastern Indiana and Northern Kentucky. If you’re along the 75 corridor up to Springboro, you’re likely in for a steady light rain, and the same applies to areas east of the city. Don’t expect any dramatic thunderstorms, but a rumble or two might still surprise us, especially further south.

Key Takeaway: Expect a wet and potentially annoying morning commute. Factor in extra travel time and drive cautiously.

Dreary Afternoon and a Glimpse of Winter

Once we get past the morning rush, the rain will start to ease off, shifting east and southeast of I-275. By lunchtime, most of the city should be drying out, but don’t expect sunshine. It’s going to remain dreary and gray with temperatures barely climbing into the low 40s. So, yeah, a cold rain to start your day and not much improvement as the day progresses. However, looking ahead, we have a potential twist – maybe, just maybe, some flurries could make an appearance first thing Friday morning for you early birds! It won’t be a snowstorm, just a brief tease of winter before it mixes into an annoying cold rain, don’t expect any accumulation. Christmas Eve and Christmas Day will likely see more rain and temperatures in the 40s. So hold off on the winter wonderland dreams for now.

Key Takeaway: Get used to gloomy skies and prepare for a cold, possibly mixed precipitation on Friday morning. Keep your umbrella close!

A Look Ahead

While the weather might not be cooperating, we can still find ways to enjoy our day. Bundle up, grab a warm drink, and maybe even find an excuse to stay inside. Whether you’re a Bengals fan hoping for a miracle playoff spot or just trying to get through your workday, stay safe out there. And remember, even on the dreariest days, there’s always something to be grateful for. So, share this update with your friends, and let’s navigate this crazy weather together! What are your plans for this rainy day? Let us know in the comments!
