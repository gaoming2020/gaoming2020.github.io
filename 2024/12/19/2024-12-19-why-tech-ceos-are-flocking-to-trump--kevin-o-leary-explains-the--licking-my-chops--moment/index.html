Why Tech CEOs are Flocking to Trump: Kevin O'Leary Explains the 'Licking My Chops' Moment

Posted on 2024-12-19

Why are some of the biggest names in tech, like the CEOs of Amazon, Netflix, Apple, and Google, suddenly making a beeline for Mar-a-Lago? It’s not just about politics, it’s about cold, hard business. As Kevin O’Leary put it, he’s “licking his own chops” at the prospect of what’s coming. Let’s break down why these CEOs are seemingly cozying up to Trump.

First off, it’s all about the numbers. Trump has a significant mandate, and that spells stability for businesses. But beyond that, CEOs are laser-focused on two critical factors: tax rates and deregulation. They’re pretty confident that the current 21% tax rate is here to stay, a much more palatable scenario than the potential 28% that had been floated. This certainty alone is a major draw. Added to that, the promise of deregulation, particularly in areas like energy and social media freedom, is like a business dream come true. For these leaders, it’s not about personal preference, it’s about anticipating and leveraging the political landscape to their advantage.

O’Leary also emphasizes that this isn’t just about sucking up to power, it’s about a strategic move. Trump’s track record is seen as pro-business, especially compared to what was perceived as damaging policies under the previous administration that focused on government spending instead of business growth. The key takeaway is that Trump’s policies seem to offer a more predictable and potentially profitable environment for businesses. As O’Leary puts it, ‘This mandate is going to be way better than the last one, a lot more stable and very good for business.’ This isn’t just a matter of speculation; it’s about the rational decisions that come with a clear majority mandate.

Ultimately, the tech CEO convergence at Mar-a-Lago speaks volumes about the business community’s desire for stability, lower taxes, and less regulation. It’s a signal that for many in the corporate world, Trump’s economic vision aligns with their goals.
