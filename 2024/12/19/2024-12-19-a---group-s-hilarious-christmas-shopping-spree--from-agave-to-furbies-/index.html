Aぇ! Group's Hilarious Christmas Shopping Spree: From Agave to Furbies!

Posted on 2024-12-19

Ever wondered what a K-pop boy group does for Christmas? Well, maybe not K-pop, but our favorite Japanese boy group, Aぇ! Group, certainly had a unique approach to Christmas gift shopping! Let me tell you, it was less about perfectly wrapped presents and more about hilarious chaotic fun. This video gave us a sneak peek into their Christmas party preparation, and honestly, it’s just as entertaining as their performances!

First off, can we talk about the sheer variety of gift ideas? We had Kojima introducing a random ‘Presley’ (we’re not quite sure what that was!), and then Suezawa, the plant dad, declaring his love for agave. Yes, you read that right - agave, as in, the spiky desert succulent. I’m just picturing someone opening that up on Christmas morning! Then there’s Sano who dove head first into the realm of self-discovery with a ‘My Birthday Book’. Apparently, you can find your personality, fortune, and even suitable jobs based on your blood type. It sounds like the perfect gift for anyone looking to start 2025 with a bang!

But the real highlight has to be Masakado and his three-minute shopping spree at Village Vanguard. I feel his pain! It’s a store that’s a treasure trove of quirky goodness, but he was in and out so fast, leaving him with a serious case of shopping remorse. Talk about a power shopper! And then, who could forget Richard’s discovery of the talking Furby? It might not be the most verbose of toys, but the sounds definitely added to the overall hilarious vibe.

Overall, this video wasn’t just about gift-giving; it’s about the genuine camaraderie and fun-loving nature of Aぇ! Group. Their shopping trip reminds us that Christmas doesn’t have to be picture-perfect; it’s about the laughter and moments shared with loved ones. If you want a dose of holiday cheer with a side of chaotic shopping, you’ll definitely want to check out the full video!
