Chilly Weather Alert: Clouds and Cooler Temps Stick Around Through the Weekend

Posted on 2024-12-19

Did you wake up to rain showers this morning? Well, get ready for a shift because we’re not just talking about a passing storm; we’re diving headfirst into some serious winter-like conditions. The rain has moved off to the east, making way for cooler, drier air, and those grey skies aren’t planning on going anywhere anytime soon.

So, what can we expect? Here are the key points:

  • Cloudy Skies Are the Norm: Those low clouds you see? They’re here to stay, at least for the rest of today and tomorrow. This cloud cover will play a major role in keeping temperatures down and will be a major factor in our weather for the next few days. Don’t expect a big temperature change as cloud cover will block any heat from the sun from reaching us. Some models suggest a potential for a little sunshine later in the day, but for now, it looks like we’re in for a cloudy spell, so be prepared.

  • Chilly Temperatures: Get your jackets ready because we’re looking at overnight lows in the low 30s. And don’t expect much improvement during the day. Temperatures will linger in the upper 30s to low 40s for the next few afternoons. Heading out to a volleyball game or some Christmas light viewing? Make sure you’re bundled up because those mid-30s aren’t exactly balmy. We also have a passing system on the way that will give us a few flurries to look forward to as well but won’t stay in the area very long.

  • Looking Ahead: While the short-term forecast is all about clouds and chill, there’s a glimmer of hope for Christmas! Things will start to get a bit milder, with temperatures potentially hitting the low 50s. Although there is some chance of showers for both Christmas Eve and Christmas Day, at least the temperatures will be more comfortable. We may have rain for Christmas instead of snow, but the kids can still enjoy their bikes and golf clubs, even with a few showers in between!

In conclusion, prepare for the winter chill to stick around. Expect cloudy skies, cooler temperatures, and a chance of a few flurries as we head into the weekend. But with a bit of luck, we’ll get a break for the holidays with some milder and possibly wetter weather coming our way. Make sure to share this post so everyone can be prepared for what the weather has in store!
