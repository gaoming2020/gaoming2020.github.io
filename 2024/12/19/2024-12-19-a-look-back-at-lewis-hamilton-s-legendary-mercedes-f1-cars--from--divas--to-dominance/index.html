A Look Back at Lewis Hamilton's Legendary Mercedes F1 Cars: From 'Divas' to Dominance

Posted on 2024-12-19

Ever wondered what happens to those Formula 1 cars after they cross the finish line? Well, Lewis Hamilton and Toto Wolff recently took a walk down memory lane, revisiting Hamilton’s championship-winning Mercedes F1 cars, and it’s a sight to behold. It’s not just about the cars; it’s a story of evolution, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

The video tour reveals not just the gleaming machines but also the subtle changes and bold leaps in design. Here are a couple key takeaways:

  • The Evolution of a Champion: The progression from the 2014 car, which Hamilton called a ‘rocket ship’, to the 2020 ‘dominant’ black machine is fascinating. The early cars look almost tiny compared to their later counterparts, with significantly narrower tires and simpler aero packages. The introduction of the halo in 2018 and the subsequent refinements are a testament to the constant push for safety and performance in F1.

  • ‘Diva’ to Dominance: The 2017 car, affectionately nicknamed ‘diva’, perfectly encapsulates the challenge of Formula 1 engineering. Despite being incredibly fast, it was notoriously difficult to handle – a testament to the delicate balance between raw power and drivability. By 2020, the team had seemingly ironed out all the creases, producing a car that was dominant and aesthetically stunning. The transition to a black livery in 2020 was not just a design choice, but a powerful statement of solidarity, making this car and moment in time even more memorable.

Beyond the cars themselves, the collection includes a full range of helmets, each telling its own story of Hamilton’s career. From the early yellow designs to the special ‘Sorayama’ collection, it’s clear this collection is a deeply personal and historical archive. Imagine seeing that all displayed in a museum?

Lewis and Toto’s stroll through this impressive collection was a reminder of their journey. As they said, it’s the end of one phase of their lives racing together from 2013. It’s an epic story of how a team managed to turn a vision into an unrivaled level of success. If you’re a fan of Formula 1, this should inspire and entertain.
