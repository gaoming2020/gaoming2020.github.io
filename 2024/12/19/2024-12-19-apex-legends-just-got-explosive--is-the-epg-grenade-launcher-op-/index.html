Apex Legends Just Got Explosive: Is the EPG Grenade Launcher OP?

Posted on 2024-12-19

Okay, Apex Legends fans, let’s talk about the EPG. Yes, that EPG – the grenade launcher we’ve been seeing in the rift is now unleashed upon the arena and it’s shaking things up. Forget careful aim; this thing is about chaotic, explosive power, and honestly? It’s a blast.

For years, this weapon seemed stuck in limbo, but no more! The EPG is available for any Legend now, and it comes packed with 28 shots of pure, splash-damage goodness. Forget precision; think area denial. Direct hit? Boom, 105 damage. Land it near their feet? Still painful at 65 damage. The further away it is from the enemy, the less damage it inflicts, but, regardless, there will be some damage dealt.

But here’s where things get really interesting. Not only is the EPG capable of obliterating enemies, but it’s also a great tool for vertical traversal. Think you’re stuck on a low-level platform? Simply aim at the ground, fire, and poof, you’re airborne! It’s like having a personal rocket jump feature. This is not just offensive; it’s game-changing in how you maneuver around the map, adding a whole new layer of strategic movement.

And if all that wasn’t enough, the EPG isn’t just for taking out enemy legends; it is also a demolition tool. Doors? Not an obstacle anymore. Just a quick shot at the wall or the ground near them, and they are no more. The destruction is real. It really adds a satisfying edge to clearing buildings.

So, is the EPG overpowered? Maybe. But that doesn’t mean it isn’t fun. It’s a chaotic, unpredictable weapon that can turn the tide of a battle in seconds. Give it a try and share your experiences in the comments. Let me know what you think of this new addition! Will you be picking it up often? Or is it not your cup of tea? Either way, I think this new addition is a major W.
