Is Fear Undermining Democracy? Raskin Warns of Retribution Against Liz Cheney and the Press

Posted on 2024-12-19

Is our democracy under attack? It might sound like a dramatic question, but recent events are raising serious concerns. Congressman Jamie Raskin suggests that we’re witnessing a concerted effort to intimidate political opposition and suppress the press. He points to the intense scrutiny of Liz Cheney, not as a simple political disagreement, but as an attempt to make an example out of her and instill fear in others.

This isn’t just about one person. Raskin emphasizes that the tactics used against Cheney – such as investigating her work on the January 6th committee – are part of a larger pattern. He argues that this pattern mirrors authoritarian regimes where opposition figures face legal threats and the press is silenced, and that is all to serve a bigger purpose of eroding democratic institutions. Here are two key takeaways:

  • Intimidation as a Strategy: Raskin argues that the targeting of Liz Cheney, a prominent Republican who stood against Trump, is a calculated move to scare others into silence. He highlights that Cheney, previously a high-ranking member of the Republican caucus, was specifically chosen to send a powerful message: dissent will be punished. This creates a chilling effect, potentially discouraging others from speaking out against the powerful, regardless of their political leaning. This fear extends beyond political figures, encompassing news outlets and anyone deemed critical.
  • The Oligarchic Threat: The Congressman also expresses deep concern about the growing influence of wealthy individuals, describing it as an “oligarchic class” trying to use its power to circumvent democratic processes. He points to figures like Elon Musk, who has actively influenced legislative outcomes, as evidence of this. This, coupled with the potential for unchecked presidential power, presents a significant challenge to the balance of power enshrined in the Constitution.

We often hear talk about checks and balances. But what happens when those checks and balances are intentionally undermined? Raskin warns that we are entering a dangerous territory, where the rule of law and the freedom of the press, fundamental tenets of our democracy, are under assault. It’s a time to pay attention and ensure that our institutions remain strong. Let’s not allow fear to dictate our future. What are your thoughts? Share this article to start a conversation.
