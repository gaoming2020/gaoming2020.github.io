From Tsunami Survivor to Guardian: One Man's Inspiring Fight to Protect His Village

Posted on 2024-12-19

Imagine surviving a catastrophe that claimed the lives of thousands, including a quarter of your own village. That’s the reality Bala Chusin faced in 2004 when a devastating tsunami struck his home in southern Thailand. But instead of succumbing to fear, Bala turned his experience into a powerful force for good. For the past 17 years, this 59-year-old former fisherman has stood as a vigilant guardian, watching the sea for any sign of another impending wave.

What’s truly remarkable is how Bala channeled his personal trauma into community action. The tsunami, triggered by a massive 9.1-magnitude earthquake, was like nothing he had ever witnessed, describing it as a “very tall wall of mist.” He thought everyone in his village would die. This harrowing experience fueled his determination to protect his community. Bala isn’t just a passive observer; he’s a key part of a system designed to safeguard Ban Nam Kham. This includes a concrete shelter, two alarm systems, and well-defined evacuation routes. He’s also entrusted to watch the waves after earthquakes and reports to the local authorities.

Beyond the infrastructure, Bala embodies the spirit of preparedness. He educates his children, grandchildren, and neighbors, urging them to learn from the past. The village is now encouraged to keep grab-bags of essential documents ready for quick evacuation. It’s about more than just physical structures; it’s about a change in mindset, a commitment to community safety, and a conscious effort to be proactive, not reactive. This dedication has helped him feel that his village is prepared. And with his 60th birthday approaching, Bala believes this will be his only tsunami experience, demonstrating resilience and the power of community.

Bala’s story is a testament to human resilience and the profound impact one individual can have. His unwavering dedication, rooted in personal tragedy, has transformed his village into a community that’s not just recovering but thriving with a sense of safety. His story reminds us that even from the darkest of times, hope and positive change can emerge. Consider sharing his story to inspire others to take action and get prepared. Do you have a plan in place for your own community?
